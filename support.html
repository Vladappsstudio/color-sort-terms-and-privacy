Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • I lost my progress. What can I do?
    Ensure you are using the same device. Currently, progress is stored locally. We recommend not clearing the app data.
  • How do I remove ads?
    You can purchase the "Remove Ads" option from the in-game Settings menu for a one-time fee.
  • The game freezes on my device.
    Please try restarting your device. If the issue persists, email us with your device model and Android version.
